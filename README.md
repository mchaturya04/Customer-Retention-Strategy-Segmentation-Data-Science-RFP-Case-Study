# Customer Retention Strategy & Segmentation  
## Data Science RFP Case Study – Acme Innovations

##  Project Overview
This project was developed as part of a Data Science RFP (Request for Proposal) competition, simulating a real-world consulting engagement for Acme Innovations — a legacy consumer products company facing declining customer retention.

The objective was to apply data analytics and data mining techniques to understand customer behavior, identify at-risk segments, and propose actionable retention and marketing strategies.

---

##  Business Problem
Acme Innovations has experienced a steady decline in customer retention due to evolving consumer preferences and increased competition. The company sought a data-driven approach to:
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Segment customers based on similarities
- Identify cross-selling and product bundling opportunities
- Improve customer retention and engagement strategies

---

##  Data Description
- Customer demographic and behavioral data
- Purchase history and transactional data
- No missing values or significant outliers identified
- Data standardized prior to modeling

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- R Programming
- RStudio
- R Markdown
- K-Means Clustering
- Apriori Algorithm
- Data Cleaning & Standardization
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Data Visualization

---

##  Methodology

### Customer Segmentation
- Applied **K-Means clustering** to group customers based on spending behavior and tenure
- Used the **Elbow Method** to determine the optimal number of clusters
- Identified 4 distinct customer segments with meaningful behavioral patterns

### Market Basket Analysis
- Implemented the **Apriori algorithm** to identify frequently purchased product combinations
- Evaluated rules using **support, confidence, and lift**
- Identified strong product affinities for cross-selling opportunities

---

##  Key Insights
- Four distinct customer segments with varying spending behaviors
- Spending score and salary explained a significant portion of customer variability
- Bluetooth speakers showed strong affinity with multiple product combinations
- Product bundling opportunities identified to increase basket size and retention

---

##  Recommendations
- Develop targeted marketing strategies for each customer segment
- Implement product recommendation and bundling strategies
- Focus retention efforts on high-value and at-risk customer clusters
- Use affinity insights to personalize promotions and discounts

---

##  Outcome
This project demonstrates the application of data analytics and data mining techniques to solve a real-world business problem. It highlights strong analytical thinking, customer segmentation skills, and the ability to translate data insights into actionable business recommendations.
